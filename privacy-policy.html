Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to INFINIPDF ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our PDF tools.

2. Data Processing

Client-Side Processing: INFINIPDF is designed to process your files locally in your browser. We do not upload your PDF files to our servers for processing. This ensures high security and privacy.

Authentication: If you choose to log in, we use Firebase (Google) to handle authentication. We do not store your passwords directly.

3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Services

We use third-party advertising companies (Google AdSense) to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies (small text files placed on your computer) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. Detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@infinipdf.com.
